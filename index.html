How to play

  1. Tap the ring or press any key to start.
  2. Watch the sequence of colors flash.
  3. Repeat it back by clicking the pads, or press 1 2 3 4 on your keyboard.
  4. Each round adds one more color to remember.
  5. Answer before the timer runs out, or it's game over.
  6. Pick a difficulty before you start to change speed and time limits.
